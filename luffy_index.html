Monkey D. luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat" Luffy, is a fictional character and the protagonist of the One Piece manga series, created by Eiichiro Oda.
Luffy made his debut as a young boy who acquires the properties of rubber after accidentally eating one of the Devil Fruits that belonged to “Red Hair” Shanks.
